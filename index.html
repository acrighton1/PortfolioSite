"Areli"

Introducing Areli, the ultimate lifestyle blog template built on the powerful Bootstrap 5 framework. This child theme is based on the bootScore 5 parent theme and offers a fully customizable design, complete with customizable colors, images, social media icons, and more. The template features a custom sidebar that displays your latest blog posts and a mini slider to showcase your top content. With animate on scroll effects using the AOS library, Areli adds a dynamic touch to your website. A special section is also included for showcasing your Instagram feed or an image carousel plugin. Areli fully utilizes the Gutenberg block editor, making it easy for you to create and manage your content. Built with HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, and Bootstrap 5, Areli is a reliable and robust choice for your lifestyle blog.
